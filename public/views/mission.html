ABOUT OUR COMPANY


ICEE Solutions LLC is a leading provider of power verification and validation products for SOC designs with multiple power domains, especially for mixed signal designs.

Today, battery powered communication devices, such as smart phones, tablets, are everywhere. With more functions integrated into one device, there are billions of transistors on a single chip, which has mixed analog/digital blocks and those blocks are working under different power domains. Analyzing and verifying the power consumption under different working modes and reducing power leakage have become very difficult issues, especially for SOC top level and analog blocks.
